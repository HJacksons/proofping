# ProofPing

ProofPing is a privacy-first real-human proof platform.

## One-liner

Need a real person to check something AI cannot?

## Product Promise

ProofPing helps adults get quick real-world confirmation from real humans for listings, sellers, shops, addresses, local situations, public places, queues, documents, and claims that AI cannot physically verify.

## Not a Q&A App

ProofPing does not compete with ChatGPT. AI can answer questions, but AI cannot physically verify a place, confirm a seller exists, check a local situation, or provide human accountability.

## Core Loop

1. User creates a proof request.
2. User shares a link.
3. A real person replies with text, photo, video, or local confirmation.
4. The requester reviews replies.
5. The request is marked solved, suspicious, unresolved, or closed.
6. Optional donation, unlock, or urgent boost.

## First Use Case

Verify before you pay.

Examples:

- Is this apartment listing real?
- Can someone near this shop confirm it is open?
- Does this address exist?
- Is this seller suspicious?
- Can someone local confirm this place looks like the photos?
- Can someone explain this notice in the local language?

## Safety Rules

- 18+ only.
- No stalking.
- No tracking private people.
- No doxxing.
- No harassment.
- No romantic surveillance.
- No requests involving children.
- No medical diagnosis.
- No illegal services.
- No public ratings of private individuals.

## Design Feeling

Welcoming, calm, trustworthy, global, simple, human, warm, and serious.

The product should feel like help from a kind local person, not like a noisy social network.
